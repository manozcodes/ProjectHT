ChampaDevi

SHORT HIKE

Kathmandu, Nepal

Destination Highlights

 This is third highest hill around Kathmandu valley, which is at the height of 2278 meter.  Enjoy fantastic aerial views of Historical places like Bugmati, Khokana, Pharping and also Bagmati River.  You can observe the bustling valley (Kathmandu, Lalitpur and Bhaktapur) and spectacular village settlements from the top.  In autumn and winter, the summit offers commanding views of the Himalayan range such as Gauri Shankar, Dorje Lakpa, Langtang Lirung, Ganesh Himal from the top.  You can see Fulchoki hill on your right, Chandra Giri which is highest hill around.  You can find varieties of Flora and Fauna in the journey.  You can visit the Champadevi Temple revered by both Hindus and Buddhists.  Well maintained walking trail with stone steps
